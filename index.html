Predicting Player Rank in Starcraft: Insights and Recommendations

This project aimed to develop a predictive model for a player's rank in Starcraft using the provided dataset. The analysis involved data preprocessing, EDA, and training multiple models. ActionLatency, APM, TotalHours, SelectByHotkeys, and AssignToHotkeys were identified as significant features. Recommendations for players include minimizing ActionLatency, increasing APM, investing more time, and mastering hotkey usage. The Random Forest model performed best, but improvements are needed. Overall, the project provided insights into rank factors and recommendations for performance enhancement.

Pedaling Towards Success: How We Used Data to Develop a Bike-Share Marketing Strategy

The bike-share analysis aims to design a new marketing strategy to convert casual riders into annual members. It involves data cleaning, manipulation, analysis, visualization, and business acumen. Key findings show differences in ride frequency, distance, length, time of day, and vehicle type usage. Based on the insights, the project recommends targeting casual riders to encourage more frequent use, marketing as a fun way to explore the city, and expanding the electric bike fleet to attract more annual members.
